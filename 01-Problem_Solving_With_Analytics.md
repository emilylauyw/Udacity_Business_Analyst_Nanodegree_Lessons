# 01 - Problem Solving with Analytics

## The Analytics Problem Solving Framework
Two key analytical concepts to understand any business situation and to choose the correct techniques to analyze the data.

### Cross Industry Standard Process for Data Mining (CRISP-DM)
This framework was originally developed by data miners in order to generalize the common approaches to defining and analyzing a problem.

The framework is made up of 6 steps:

1. Business Issue Understanding
2. Data Understanding
3. Data Preparation
4. Analysis/Modeling
5. Validation
6. Presentation/Visualization
